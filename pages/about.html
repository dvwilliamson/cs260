About the Author

My name is Dallas Williamson. I am a senior studying computer science in Brigham Young University. I believe strongly that anyone can do anything that they put their minds to. I made this site because after learning Spanish, I realized that it is just as difficult to learn a new language for the first time, as it is for learning programming langauges. I also realized that after learning several langauges, it becomes much eaiser to learn more. With that in mind, I hope that this website can help those who are learning to speak new languages, as well as those who are learning to program in new languages.
